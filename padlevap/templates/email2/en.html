Most players waste time stretching—pros do this 5-minute routine to stay agile and avoid tears:

  • Dynamic Lunges (3 sets, 10 reps) – Warms up legs and improves lateral movement.
  • Shoulder “Band Pulls” (2 mins) – Prevents rotator cuff strains.
  • Ball-Tap Drills (1 min) – Sharpens hand-eye coordination fast.

Bonus Tip: Never static stretch before playing—it weakens muscles! Save it for post-match.
